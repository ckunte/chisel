{% markdown %} ## Biography Chetan Kunté is an offshore structures & turret-mooring engineer. His professional areas of interest include structural mechanics, hydrodynamics, fatigue, and offshore geotechnics in applications ranging from fixed & floating steel structures, hulls & ship-shaped vessels, turrets & mooring systems, tendons & risers, fenders, dolphins & jetties. He enjoys cycling, nonfiction literature, and programming in python. ### Appointments held
