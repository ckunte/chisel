Professional {% markdown %} ## Biography Chetan Kunte is an offshore structures engineer at __RDS__. His professional areas of interest include structural mechanics, hydrodynamics, fatigue, and offshore geotechnics in applications ranging from fixed and floating steel structures, hulls and ship-shaped vessels, turrets and mooring systems, tendons and risers, fenders, dolphins and jetties. He enjoys cycling, nonfiction literature, and programming in python. ### Appointments held
