About

{% markdown %} Hello, I am [Chetan Kunte][bio]. This is a personal web site,[^1] where I [log][a] my thoughts on engineering problems I’m trying to solve, side projects I am working on, re-tooling, and about things I find interesting. ### Colophon I built __ckunte.net__ with [Chisel][c]. It respects web standards and produces valid [HTML][v1], [CSS][v2], and [RSS][v3] markups. It is phone friendly too. Keyboard shortcuts are available for site navigation. On MacOS, please use the combination: `Ctrl` `Alt` `Key`.
