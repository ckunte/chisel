About

{% markdown %} I am an offshore structures engineer, and this little website is my sandbox. Here I love to code, experiment, write. Currently, I live in Perth. __ckunte.net__ exists mainly to be a pointer to an __MX__ record. I put the idling __A__ record to use, mostly as an afterthought, which turned-on the lights here. Built with [Chisel][c1], this site respects web standards, produces valid [HTML][v1], [CSS][v2], [RSS][v3] markups, and is phone-friendly. Handy keyboard shortcuts are available as below. There are no forms seeking visitor inputs, and there are no advertisements, no cookies, and no trackers served from this website. This is a deliberate effort as the site author to not seek this private data from site visitors. The story, however, does not end here. It would be prudent to assume GitHub, jsDelivr, and Netlify, who provide infrastructural service in hosting this site (as repository, CDN service for KaTeX, and web service), may be recording visits to this site on their servers. I have no access, no visibility, and no inclination to find out what data is being recorded from such visits. __Take charge.__ To avoid revealing inadvertently about your computer, your habits, browser plug-ins/extensions (e.g. ad-blocker, password manager, etc.) or even about yourself via your browser, I strongly recommend using privacy protecting browsers (e.g., Safari and Brave).[^1] I also strongly recommend using network-wide blockers (e.g., [pi-hole][ph]) to block ads and trackers in your household and workplaces. These help in stopping the unwanted and the underbelly of the internet — bent on recording personal and private information unsolicited — from loading up on your computers and devices. Aside from using numerous community block-lists, I also maintain a [regex block list for pi-hole][bl] that enables wide blocking. Consider building one for your needs based on what tools like pi-hole pick-up. On the go, redirect internet traffic via a network-wide blocker enabled LAN by using a VPN service like [Tailscale][ts]. Let’s make unsolicited visitor data recording a futile effort for all parties. ## Disclaimer, Terms of use I do not speak for my employer.
